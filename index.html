Welcome Drink

Begin your feast with a splash of refreshment. Our specially prepared welcome drink is designed to cleanse your palate and set a delightful tone for the evening's culinary journey.

Tomato Shorba

A velvety and aromatic soup, rich with the flavor of ripe tomatoes and infused with a delicate blend of Indian spices. It's the perfect, heartwarming prelude to the main course.

Chicken Biriyani

The crown jewel of our Eid feast. Succulent pieces of chicken marinated in spices and layered with fragrant, long-grain basmati rice, slow-cooked to perfection. An unforgettable experience.

Payasam

End your meal on a sweet note with this classic South Indian dessert. A creamy, slow-simmered pudding, delicately flavored with cardamom and garnished with nuts for a truly indulgent finish.

And Your Friday Favourites!

In addition to our Eid specials, the dinner will also feature the complete, delicious spread you know and love from our regular Friday night menu. There's something for everyone to enjoy!
